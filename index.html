HOW TO USE
1. Power on your Arduino (plug into power bank)
2. Wait for 3 LED blinks on Arduino
3. Tap CONNECT on this app
4. Tap START SESSION before hitting
5. Hit your shots — swings auto detected
6. Tap STOP SESSION when done
7. Go to SESSIONS to export your data
DATA FORMAT
ax — X acceleration (g)
ay — Y acceleration (g)
az — Z acceleration (g)
gx — X gyroscope (°/s)
gy — Y gyroscope (°/s)
gz — Z gyroscope (°/s)
mag — Total magnitude
